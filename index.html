Membership Eligibility

Eligible students are invited based on GPA standing within their class. Invitations are sent each academic year — check your UCI email. Graduate students and faculty may also be inducted.

Get Involved

Free Tutoring

  • Drop-in sessions for lower & upper-div ECE/CS courses
  • Staffed by active HKN members
  • Held weekly in the Engineering buildings
  • No appointment needed — just show up

Events & Activities

  • Induction ceremonies each academic year
  • Industry networking nights & company tours
  • Resume workshops and mock interviews
  • Social events and community outreach

Member Benefits

  • Official IEEE-HKN membership certificate
  • Access to exclusive alumni network
  • Priority for research and internship referrals
  • Leadership opportunities as chapter officer
